About

ABBARAMA is a group of young musicians from Sweden, UK and USA celebrating the music and style of ABBA live.

The show is performed with a modern electronic sound produced by Grammy award winning producer Greg Collins (U2, No Doubt) along with art and video projections by LA pop artist Kii Arens (Lady Gaga, Katy Perry) creating the ultimate ABBA experience.
